COLLEGE EXPECTATIONS VS REALITY

EXPECTATIONS

* BIG CAMPUS

* BIG SPORTS GROUND

* NO ATTENDANCE PROBLEM

* GOOD AREA AROUND CAMPUS

* HIGH SPEED INTERNET

* NO RESTRICTIONS

* HIGH PACKAGE PLACEMENT

* NO TENSIONS FOR EXAMS

REALITY

* SMALL TEMPORARY CAMPUS

* NOT SO GOOD FACILITIES FOR SPORTS

* 80 PERCENTAGE ATTENDANCE MANDATORY

* LESS SHOPS AROUND CAMPUS

* NO HIGH SPEED INTERNET

* RESTRICTIONS ON EVERY ACTIVITIES

* NOT TO MUCH HIGH PACKAGE

* MUCH PRESSURE FOR EXAMS
